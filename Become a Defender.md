# Become a Defender - TryHackMe Writeup

## 🧠 Overview
In this room, I explored the fundamentals of defensive security, learned how to identify and understand client infrastructure, and developed a defender mindset to anticipate how attacks may occur.

By thinking like both an attacker and a defender, I examined how security controls can be applied across multiple layers to reduce risk and protect important systems.

---

## 🛡️ What I Did

- Explored how defenders analyze and protect systems from potential threats  
- Learned how infrastructure can be monitored and secured  
- Participated in a simulator that represented defending a city infrastructure, drawing parallels to real-world cybersecurity defense  

---

## ⚠️ Challenges

- Adapting to a defensive mindset instead of thinking purely like an attacker  
- Understanding how different layers of security work together in practice  

---

## 💡 Key Takeaways

- Defensive security focuses on prevention, detection, and response  
- Thinking like an attacker helps in identifying weaknesses  
- Security is layered, and each layer plays a role in protecting systems  

---

## 🚀 Reflection

This room marks the completion of the Pre Security learning path. I now have a foundational understanding of core security concepts from both offensive and defensive perspectives.

This experience helped me see cybersecurity not just as attacking systems, but also as protecting and maintaining them.
