***Understanding the CIA (Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability) Triad and how it shapes cyber security mindset was the key topic of this room.***

**Learning Objectives**:
Understanding the pillars of cyber security, the purpose of Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability, while recognizing them in simple scenarios and making decisions to preserve these core aspects of cyber security.

Understanding the CIA TRIAD:

Confidentintiality means, you own your data, and no one can have the access, unless you allow them to.
Integrity means, your data was not modified without your permissions, and remains as original as it was, when you released it.
Availability refers to the state that you have maximum uptime at your need, and preventing downtime, through security measures.

The CIA Triad is a security mindset.
